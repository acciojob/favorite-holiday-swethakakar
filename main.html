Paris


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its iconic landmarks, rich history, and vibrant culture. Whether you're exploring world-famous museums, strolling along the Seine River, or enjoying delicious French pastries, Paris offers a little something for everyone.

  1. Eiffel Tower - t1 One of the most recognizable landmarks in the world.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to thousands of artworks, including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - t2A stunning example of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre - t3 A historic district offering spectacular views of the city.
  5. Champs-Élysées - t4A grand avenue perfect for shopping and sightseeing.
  • Croissant - t1A buttery, flaky pastry, perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot - t2 A classic French dish of snails cooked with garlic butter.
  • Macaron - t3A sweet, colorful meringue-based treat.
  • Ratatouille - t4A flavorful vegetable stew, often served as a side dish.
  • Crepes - t5 A thin, versatile pancake, often served with sweet or savory fillings.
